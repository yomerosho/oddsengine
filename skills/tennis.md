# Tennis Betting Skill (Reference)

## Core Concepts
- **Serve hold % / break %**: Foundation of tennis math
- **Surface specialization** — clay, hard, grass are different sports
- **First serve %, win % on 1st serve**
- **Break points saved/converted**
- **Head-to-head** matters more in tennis than most sports

## Surface Tendencies
- **Clay** (French Open, Monte Carlo, Madrid, Rome): 
  Slower, longer rallies, baseline grinders dominate
- **Hard** (US Open, Australian Open, most Masters): 
  Balanced, all-court games shine
- **Grass** (Wimbledon, Queens): Fast, low bounce, 
  big servers dominate
- **Indoor hard**: Even faster than outdoor hard, 
  rewards aggression

## Prop Drivers
1. Surface fit
2. Recent form (last 10 matches on this surface)
3. Travel & altitude (Madrid altitude favors big servers)
4. Best-of-3 vs. best-of-5 (Slams only)
5. Physical condition (back-to-back matches in heat)
6. Mental toughness (clutch break point conversion)

## Key Situational Factors
- **First-round Slam matches**: Variance high, lines softer
- **Grand Slam quarter+ rounds**: Sharp markets
- **Davis Cup / United Cup**: Different motivation
- **Late-season fatigue (October-November)**: Top players 
  ease up
- **Olympic year**: Schedule disruption

## Common Markets
- **Match winner (moneyline)**
- **Set betting (3-0, 3-1, 3-2 etc.)**
- **Total games over/under**
- **Total sets (2.5 / 3.5 / 4.5 in slams)**
- **Player to win a set (for big underdogs)**

## Information Sources
- **Tennis Abstract (tennisabstract.com)** — ELO ratings, 
  match logs
- **ATP/WTA official sites**
- **Tennis Twitter**: @TennisAbstract, @bencrosaries 
  (Ben Rothenberg)
- **Match Stat (matchstat.com)** — H2H, surface splits

## Edge Spots
- Surface specialists at non-favored events (clay-courter 
  on clay, soft line)
- Big-server underdogs at fast-court tournaments
- 2nd-round opponents of top seeds (after seed wins easy 1st)
- Live betting on serve break momentum (advanced)

## Caution
- **Limited prop markets** vs. major US sports
- **Lower limits** on books — lines move fast
- **Retirement risk** in long matches — affects live bets
- **Best-of-5 variance higher** — five-set marathons swing
