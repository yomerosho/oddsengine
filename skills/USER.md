## DFS Pick'em Specific Rules
- Slip sizing rule (scales inversely with leg count):
  - 2-leg slips: max 2% of bankroll ($2 at current 
    bankroll)
  - 3-leg slips: max 1.5% of bankroll ($1.50)
  - 4-leg slips: max 1% of bankroll ($1)
  - 5-leg slips: max 0.5% of bankroll ($0.50)
  - 6-leg slips: max 0.25% of bankroll ($0.25 — 
    lottery tier only)
- Every leg must have ≥ 4% projected edge vs. sharp market
- No more than 2 legs from the same game (correlation cap)
- No more than 1 leg per player across active slips
- Always check projections within 1 hour of lock 
  (lines update as injury news breaks)
- Demon plays (PrizePicks): max 1 per slip, only if 
  edge > 6%
- Goblin plays: only if line is so soft it's 
  effectively free
- Underdog Flex/insured: only when comfort with 1 leg 
  missing matches the reduced payout
- Cross-platform check: if PrizePicks has Player X over 
  24.5 and Underdog has same player over 25.5, take the 
  better line
- No slips on games with starting lineup uncertainty 
  (NBA back-to-backs with load management risk, weather 
  games in NFL, MLB rainout risk)
