# NFL Betting Skill

## Core Metrics
- **EPA/play (Expected Points Added)**: Best single measure 
  of offensive/defensive efficiency
- **Success rate**: % of plays that gain positive EPA
- **DVOA (Football Outsiders)**: Defense-adjusted Value Over 
  Average
- **PFF grades**: Player-level performance grades
- **Pass rate over expected (PROE)**: Tendency to pass 
  vs. league norm in similar situations
- **Yards per route run (YPRR)**: WR efficiency metric
- **Pressure rate**: QB pressure faced/created

## Prop Drivers (in priority order)
1. **Game script projection** — leading teams run, trailing 
   teams pass
2. **Snap share & route participation** — playing time first
3. **Target share & air yards** — for receivers
4. **Red zone usage** — for TD props
5. **Opponent defensive matchup** (vs. position, not overall)
6. **Weather** — wind > 15mph, rain, snow affects passing

## Key Situational Factors
- **Game total & spread** — implies game script
- **Pace of play** — fast offenses = more plays = more stats
- **Weather** (outdoor games):
  - Wind > 15mph: passing yards under, kicker unders
  - Heavy rain/snow: total under, rushing over
  - Cold (< 32°F): passing efficiency drops
- **Coaching tendencies** — pass-heavy vs. run-heavy 
  play-callers
- **Injuries to OL/DL** — affects pressure & run game
- **Short week (Thursday games)** — sloppy play, totals 
  often under
- **West Coast → East Coast 1pm games** — early kickoff 
  fatigue
- **Divisional games** — typically lower scoring than 
  expected

## Prop-Specific Notes

### Passing Yards
- QB attempts × YPA — focus on attempts (game script)
- Trailing teams pass more → take overs in dog spots
- Weather (wind especially) is biggest under signal
- Check OL pressure rate vs. opposing pass rush

### Rushing Yards
- RB carries × YPC — carries is the volume signal
- Leading teams run more → take overs in favorite spots
- Game script is everything for RBs
- Goal-line back vs. workhorse — check carry distribution

### Receiving Yards / Receptions
- Target share is king
- Air yards (WR) vs. YAC (slot) — different prop profiles
- Slot WRs have higher reception floors, lower yard ceilings
- Check shadow CB matchups (e.g., elite CB on WR1)

### Anytime TD
- Goal-line carry share + red zone target share
- Vegas implied team total > 24 = TD prop value rises
- TE TDs are highly variant — only when red zone role 
  is clear

### Longest Reception / Rush
- Highly variant — needs explicit explosive play context
- Air yards (WR) and yards before contact (RB) matter

- **NFL Next Gen Stats** — speed, separation, completion%

- **Lineups.com** — inactives & snap projections

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Ignoring weather on outdoor games
- Trusting QB props in pass-heavy game scripts that don't 
  materialize
- Betting RB overs in projected blowout against (loses 
  carries when trailing)
- Stacking opposing offensive players (negative correlation 
  in low-scoring scripts)
- Overweighting last week's performance (1-game samples)

## Edge Spots
- Outdoor games with high wind — fade passing overs
- Teams as 7+ point favorites — RB rushing overs (game script)
- Teams as 7+ point dogs — QB passing overs (catch-up mode)
- WR1 vs. zone-heavy defense (no shadow CB)
- TE in red zone with WR1 injured
- Backup RB starting — line often lags volume

## Season Calendar
- August: Preseason (avoid — starters play minimally)
- September–January: Regular season (17 games)
- January–February: Playoffs (rotations tighten, sample 
  sizes shrink)
- Bye weeks: Affects rest advantages
