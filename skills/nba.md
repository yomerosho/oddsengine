# NBA Betting Skill

## Core Metrics
- **Pace**: Possessions per 48 min — directly affects all 
  counting stats (points, rebounds, assists)
- **ORTG/DRTG**: Points scored/allowed per 100 possessions
- **Net Rating**: ORTG - DRTG (true team strength)
- **Usage Rate (USG%)**: % of team plays a player uses
- **True Shooting % (TS%)**: Efficiency including 3PT and FT
- **Effective FG% (eFG%)**: FG% adjusted for 3PT value

## Prop Drivers (in priority order)
1. **Minutes projection** — biggest single driver of all 
   counting stats
2. **Usage rate** — how often does this player touch the ball
3. **Pace of game** — fast game = more counting stats for all
4. **Opponent defensive rating by position** — which positions 
   does the opponent let score?
5. **Recent form (5-10 game window)** — but discount if outlier
6. **Rest days** — back-to-backs reduce minutes & efficiency

## Key Situational Factors
- **Back-to-back (B2B)**: Star players often rest 2nd night, 
  minutes capped, props go under
- **Load management**: Check official injury report 
  (released ~3 hours before tip on NBA app)
- **Blowout risk**: If spread > 10, bench minutes increase 
  → starter props go under
- **Garbage time**: Affects bench player overs (rare hit)
- **Revenge games / national TV**: Marginal bias, mostly noise
- **Playoff series adjustments**: Rotations tighten by Game 3, 
  bench minutes drop

## Prop-Specific Notes

### Points
- Most variance — driven by shot volume + efficiency
- Check defender matchup (e.g., star vs. elite stopper)
- 3PM-dependent props have highest variance

### Rebounds
- Pace + total rebound share (TRB%)
- Matchup against weak rebounding team = boost
- Watch for blowout — bench minutes hurt rebound props

### Assists
- Pace-up + teammate efficiency = assists boost
- Star teammate out = lead guard assist boost
- Defensive matchup matters less than for points

### 3PM
- Highest variance prop — be cautious
- Check 3PA volume more than 3P%
- Pace + opponent 3PT defense

### PRA (Points + Rebounds + Assists)
- Combo props are often softest on DFS pick'em
- Look for star players in pace-up spots
- Avoid in blowout-risk games

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Betting overs because "this guy is a star" without 
  checking minutes
- Ignoring B2B fatigue
- Trusting season averages in early-season (small sample)
- Betting both player overs and team total under in same slip 
  (negative correlation)
- Stacking 3+ players from same team in DFS (correlation 
  killer in blowouts)

## Edge Spots
- Star player returns from injury — line lags actual minutes
- Blowout-risk games — fade starter overs, take bench overs
- Pace-up matchups (top 5 vs. top 5 pace) — game total + 
  star props over
- Backup PG starting (injury) — assist prop over often soft
- Garbage-time-prone teams (bad defenses, slow pace) — 
  starter overs trickier

## Season Calendar
- October–April: Regular season (82 games)
- April–June: Playoffs (rotations tighten, edge shifts)
- Summer League (July): Limited markets, avoid
- Preseason: Avoid — lineups meaningless
