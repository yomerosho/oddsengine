# NHL Betting Skill (Reference)

## Core Metrics
- **xG (Expected Goals)**: Shot quality-adjusted goal expectation
- **Corsi / Fenwick**: Shot attempt differentials (possession proxy)
- **PDO**: Shooting% + Save% (regresses to 100 — luck indicator)
- **High-danger chances (HDCF)**: Quality scoring chances
- **PP% / PK%**: Power play and penalty kill efficiency

## Prop Drivers
1. Goalie matchup (biggest single factor)
2. Line combinations (top-line ice time)
3. Power play time on ice (PP TOI)
4. Back-to-back fatigue (especially for goalies)
5. Shooting/save percentage regression candidates (PDO)

## Key Situational Factors
- **Back-to-backs**: Backup goalie often starts 2nd night
- **Travel**: West-to-east trips brutal in NHL
- **Trade deadline (March)**: Roles shift fast
- **Empty net situations**: Affects late-game props

## Common Prop Types
- Shots on goal (best volume prop)
- Points (G + A)
- Goals scored (highly variant)
- Goalie saves (depends on opponent shot volume)

## Edge Spots
- Backup goalie starting (line slow to update)
- High-PDO team facing low-PDO team (regression spot)
- Pace-up matchups for shot props
